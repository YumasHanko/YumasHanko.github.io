Method of Using the Voicebank:
      This voicebank utilizes Romanized notations. Please convert the lyrics into Romanized VCV format in the UTAU editor before inputting.
      Breathing sounds are available in five different types, numbered 1-5, corresponding to the five vowels A E I O U, followed by breathing sounds. In the UTAU editor, use "breath1, breath2, ..., breath5" to call these breathing sounds.
      To trigger vocal fry, use "parenthesis + vowel" or "vowel + parenthesis" (no spaces in between). A parenthesis before a vowel indicates the vocal fry effect comes before, and after indicates the sound comes after. For example, ")a" represents an "ah" sound with a preceding vocal fry, while "a(" represents an "ah" sound with a vocal fry following it.
